---
description: Initiates the planning and clarification phase for a new task or vague requirement.
---

1. Ask the user what feature, bugfix, or refactor they want to implement.
2. Read the system map and feature maps under `.agents/` to check affected components and dependencies.
3. Formulate a structured plan containing:
   - Your interpretation of the task.
   - Files to modify, create, or delete.
   - Clarifying questions about design, logic, or edge cases.
4. Present this proposal and wait for the user's feedback or approval before writing any code.
